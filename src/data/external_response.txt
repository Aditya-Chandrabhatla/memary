Harry Potter is the main character in the Harry Potter book series written by J.K. Rowling. He is a young wizard who discovers his magical abilities and attends Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. He is known for his lightning-shaped scar on his forehead, which he received as a baby when the dark wizard Lord Voldemort tried to kill him. Harry is also known for his bravery, loyalty, and determination to defeat Voldemort and protect his friends and loved ones.
